# Contributing to GKOS

GKOS welcomes criticism, implementation reports, examples, fixtures, and amendment proposals. Participation does not grant authority to bind the standard.

Use GitHub Discussions for exploratory questions and amendment concepts. Use issues for actionable ambiguities, defects, implementation reports, or fixture requests. Use pull requests for concrete replacement text or artifacts. Report security-sensitive information through the private process in `SECURITY.md`.

## Pre-v1.0 development status

GKOS v0.x is a testing and refinement series. Merged changes are development decisions by the Founder and Initial Editor after documented review. They are not consensus ratifications or independent certifications. Contributors and maintainers MUST describe them accordingly.

## Change classes

- Editorial
- Clarification
- Normative compatible
- Breaking
- Constitutional
- Security emergency

A normative-compatible proposal must identify affected sections and decisions, evidence, exact language, compatibility impact, security/privacy effects, fixture implications, and rollback or supersession route.

A proposal author may not describe their own work as independently approved or certified. During v0.x, the Founder and Initial Editor may adopt a proposal as a disclosed development decision under `GOVERNANCE.md`.

## Licensing and DCO

Documentation contributions are submitted under CC BY 4.0. Software-oriented contributions are submitted under Apache-2.0.

All commits intended for merge MUST carry a Developer Certificate of Origin 1.1 sign-off using `git commit -s`. The sign-off certifies that the contributor has the right to submit the contribution under the applicable license. No CLA is currently required.
