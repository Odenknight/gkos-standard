# GKOS governance

## Purpose

This document governs amendment, review, publication, and repository authority during the v0.x pre-standard period.

## Initial authority

Shaun “Oden” Marshall serves as Founder and Initial Editor. Repository authority is bounded by the published standard, decision history, applicable law, security restrictions, and the rule that no actor may approve, review, authorize, or certify its own work.

## Amendment path

1. Discussion or classified issue.
2. Evidence-backed proposal.
3. Classification as editorial, clarification, normative, breaking, constitutional, or security emergency.
4. Public review appropriate to the class.
5. Recorded disposition and authority receipt.
6. Pull request and validation.
7. Merge and changelog entry.

Normative changes require a Decision Record. Rejected proposals remain preserved. Security emergencies may impose temporary fail-closed restrictions and require retrospective review.

## Conflicts and recusal

Reviewers must disclose material conflicts. A proposer may explain and revise a proposal but may not independently approve or certify it.

## Canonical publication

The canonical v0.75 repository is `Odenknight/gkos-standard`. Migration requires a recorded governance decision and preservation of release history.
