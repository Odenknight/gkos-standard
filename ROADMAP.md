# GKOS roadmap

## v0.75 — Initial public pre-standard

Published the normative model, decision history, governance process, provisional conformance profiles, examples, graphics, and known limitations.

## v0.76 — Concept refinement and implementability clarification

- Freeze the epistemic vocabulary directly in GKOS.
- Define consequential use, blast radius, materially equivalent proposals, and defect-badge-or-refuse behavior.
- Add progressive-disclosure requirements.
- Clarify development governance and version relationships.
- Publish provisional authority-receipt fields and layer-artifact mapping.
- Keep engine and Specialist Reviewer designs as standalone informative references.

## v0.8–v0.9

- Complete machine-readable layer artifacts and OKP-CH schemas.
- Build executable GKOS-TS fixtures.
- Define stable namespaces and serialization rules.
- Define the authority-receipt mechanism, actor identity, upward attestation chain, and decision-record integrity.
- Add signed release manifests and archival identifiers.
- Conduct legal, security, privacy, records-management, and independent implementer review.
- Continue assembling the multi-stakeholder reviewer and governance body.

## v1.0 gates

- Second independent implementation.
- Demonstrated exchange and diagnostic interoperability.
- Complete conformance suite.
- Formal amendment authority and multi-stakeholder governance model.
- Voting, quorum, recusal, appeal, and dominance safeguards.
- Trademark/name administration and final brand guide.
- Canonical signed publication and persistent archival identifier.
